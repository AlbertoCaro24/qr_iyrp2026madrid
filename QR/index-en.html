INTERNATIONAL YEAR OF RANGELANDS AND PASTORALISTS (IYRP) 2026

The United Nations General Assembly has declared 2026 the International Year of Rangelands and Pastoralists (IYRP2026), a global initiative led by FAO to highlight the importance of pastoralism and rangelands for ecosystem sustainability, global food security, climate change adaptation and mitigation, and rural economic development. The initiative was promoted by the Government of Mongolia and already has the support of 102 countries and 365 organizations. From Fundación Entretantos and the Platform for Extensive Livestock and Pastoralism, we support and accompany the celebration of this International Year in its implementation in Spain.
